About GreenScape Lagos

Creating beautiful outdoor spaces for homes and businesses across Lagos

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Adebayo Johnson, GreenScape Lagos began with a simple mission: to bring the beauty of nature to urban spaces in Lagos. With over 15 years of experience in horticulture and landscape design, Adebayo assembled a team of passionate professionals dedicated to creating stunning outdoor environments.

Today, we're proud to be Lagos' most trusted landscaping company, having completed over 500 projects for residential homes, corporate offices, hotels, and government facilities across Nigeria.
